Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy describes how BookWorm collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account: username, email address, and encrypted password.
  • User content: reading updates, posts, Originals, Instants, and reviews you choose to share.
  • Usage data: in-app interactions (likes, follows, reading sessions) to show you relevant content.
  • Photos and images: only those you actively upload. We do not access your gallery without explicit permission.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the application.
  • To personalize your reading experience.
  • To show you content from people you follow.
  • To review reports and keep the community safe.

3. Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal information for advertising purposes. We use the following infrastructure services:

  • Supabase – database and authentication.
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In – optional authentication.
  • Expo / EAS – application distribution.

4. Content You Publish

Content you publish on BookWorm is visible to other users. By publishing, you confirm that you hold the rights to that content. You retain full intellectual property ownership of everything you upload.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete Account. Your personal data will be removed from our servers. Some backup copies may take up to 30 days to be fully deleted.

6. Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Data access is restricted through database-level security policies.

7. Children

BookWorm is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes within the app.

9. Contact

Questions: support.bookworm@gmail.com
